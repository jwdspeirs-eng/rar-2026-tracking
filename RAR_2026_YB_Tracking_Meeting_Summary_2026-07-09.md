**Date:** July 9, 2026, 7:30 AM PDT
**Attendees:** Jack Speirs, Michael Strumberger, Lee Gallacher (Ground Control / YB Tracking), Sebastien Gouin-Davis

## Race Overview

- Race Around the Rock: 4th edition, coastal rowing, full circumnavigation of Salt Spring Island
- ~88 km total course, always clockwise
- 6 checkpoints (down from 7); relay is optional, crews can swap any way they want
- Two mandatory stops: Burgoyne Bay and Welbury Bay (time check only, no required exit)
- Time cutoff at second-to-last checkpoint: 8.5 hours
- Clock runs continuously, no stops at checkpoints
- Biggest registration year yet: 10–14 teams expected, all quads and doubles so far, solo craft applications now accepted
- Hard entry deadline: 9th August

## Gate and Checkpoint Configuration

- Mandatory stops (Burgoyne, Welbury): gates run parallel to shore, close in, across bay entrance
  - Boats will pass through twice (in and out); YB tracks both entry and exit
  - Burgoyne: north-south line across the bay, close to the sandbank on the southern edge
  - Welbury: similar narrow-bay configuration, bisect the mouth
- Optional checkpoints: perpendicular gates extending well out from shore
  - All boats pass the line regardless of whether they stop; no missed gates
  - Track is tight since competitors minimize distance
- No gate width limitation: YB has used gates spanning South America to Africa
- Timing: continuous clock only, no split times at checkpoints; no value in separating stopover time

## YB Tracker Setup and Race Viewer

- Tracking interval: positions every 5 minutes, transmitted in batches of 3 every 15 minutes
- Race viewer features:
  - Embeddable iframe for the race website
  - YouTube live stream embed (auto-notifies viewers when live)
  - Boat icons reflect crew size (solo, double, quad, etc.)
  - Safety boats and committee boats displayed with distinct icons and colors
  - Filter by division/class/tag
  - Team photos, sponsor logos (high-res, transparent, priority-ordered), social media links all configurable
- Fleet sheet: boat name, team/owner name, bib/boat number, division, crew size, nationality/flag
  - Deadline: close of business Thursday before race weekend (Saturday start)
  - Latest possible: 6 hours before start
- Trackers: 18 units including spares; plan to put trackers on safety boats too

## Logistics and Shipping

- Trackers ship 10th August (day after entry deadline)
- Normal shipping to Victoria: ~3 days; with customs delays up to ~6 days
- 13-day buffer before race: sufficient even with delays
- On receipt: test and configuration required to confirm no trackers were activated in transit
